FUNCTIONALITY:-
  • Google Maps is a comprehensive mapping service that offers a wide range of functionalities. Here are some of the major features of Google Maps
    • INTERACTIVE MAPS:
  • Google Maps provides detailed and interactive maps that allow you to view locations at various levels of zoom. You can switch between map view, satellite view, and street view.
    • DIRECTIONS AND NAVIGATIONS:
  • Google Maps offers directions for driving, walking, cycling, and public transportation. It provides turn-by-turn navigation with real-time traffic information, alternate routes, and estimated arrival times.
    • LOCATION SEARCH:
  • You can search for specific addresses, landmarks, businesses, or points of interest. Google Maps also suggests popular or nearby places based on your current location.
  • Street View: With Google Maps' Street View feature, you can explore 360-degree panoramic views of streets, landmarks, and locations around the world. It gives you a ground-level perspective of various places.
    • BUSINESS LISTINGS:
  • Google Maps includes a vast database of businesses, including restaurants, hotels, shops, and services. You can search for specific businesses, read reviews, view ratings, and get information like contact details, opening hours, and photos.
    • EXPLORE AND DISCOVER:
  • Google Maps offers an "Explore" feature that helps you discover nearby places of interest, such as restaurants, cafes, parks, shopping malls, and tourist attractions. You can filter your search results based on categories and browse popular areas.
  • Saved Places and Lists: You can save specific locations, create personalized lists, and organize them into categories. This feature allows you to keep track of your favorite places, plan trips, and share recommendations with others.
    • LIVE TRAFFIC UPDATES:
  • Google Maps utilizes real-time data to provide traffic information, including congestion, accidents, and construction zones. It suggests alternative routes to avoid heavy traffic and provides estimated travel times.
  • Indoor Maps: For certain large buildings, airports, shopping malls, and transit stations, Google Maps offers indoor maps that help you navigate within these locations. It includes information about floors, stores, and amenities.
